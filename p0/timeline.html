Timeline of Elden Ring

Explore the major events and lore of The Lands Between.

The Arrival of the Greater Will

The Greater Will, a cosmic force of control, arrives in The Lands Between. It establishes the Elden Ring, a fundamental concept that governs the natural laws of life and death.

Queen Marika Becomes the Vessel

Queen Marika is chosen as the vessel for the Elden Ring, establishing the Golden Order. With her divine power, she ushers in an era of peace and grace in The Lands Between.

Godfrey’s Conquests

Godfrey, the first Elden Lord and Marika's consort, leads a series of conquests to unite The Lands Between under the Golden Order, defeating enemies like the Storm Lord and the Giants.

Radagon Marries Rennala

Radagon, a great champion, marries Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon, forming an alliance between the Carian Royals and the Golden Order. They bear three children: Ranni, Radahn, and Rykard.

The Shattering of the Elden Ring

Queen Marika, in a moment of rebellion, shatters the Elden Ring. This act leads to the fragmentation of the world’s order and sparks a civil war among her demigod children.

The Night of Black Knives

On this fateful night, assassins from the Eternal City use stolen fragments of the Rune of Death to kill Godwyn the Golden. This event marks the first death of a demigod and plunges The Lands Between into chaos.

The Tarnished Return

The Tarnished, warriors exiled from The Lands Between long ago, return following the guidance of Grace. They seek to restore the Elden Ring and become Elden Lord.
