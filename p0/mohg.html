The Lord of Blood

Mohg, the Omen and twin brother of Morgott, sought power not from the Erdtree, but from the Blood God, an outer deity. Shunned from birth due to his Omen curse, he found solace and strength in the power of blood. His rebellion against the Golden Order led him to create the Mohgwyn Dynasty, a dark realm fueled by his ambition and blood rituals. Mohg’s tale is one of vengeance, dark power, and a lust for godhood, as he seeks to build a new order under his rule.
