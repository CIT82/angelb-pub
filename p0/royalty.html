Queen of the Full Moon

Rennala of the Full Moon

A Sorceress of Unrivaled Power

Rennala, Queen of the Carian Royal Family, once ruled over the Academy of Raya Lucaria. Revered for her mastery over moon sorcery, she remains one of the most formidable magical figures in all of The Lands Between. Her teachings paved the way for many great sorcerers, and her wisdom is deeply tied to the celestial mysteries.

Renowned Sorceress

Rennala's mastery of the Full Moon spell is legendary. Her teachings continue to inspire the sorcerers of Raya Lucaria.

Celestial Wisdom

The Queen's vast knowledge of the cosmos and the stars is unmatched, giving her an edge in the art of sorcery.

Eternal Lunar Princess

Ranni, Daughter of Rennala

The Witch of the Night

Ranni, the enigmatic daughter of Rennala, renounced her royal lineage to pursue a mysterious path of her own. A master of sorcery and the moon's arcane power, Ranni seeks to forge a world free from the meddling of the gods. Her journey is one of rebellion, secrecy, and untold power.

Lunar Sorcery

Ranni's powers, deeply connected to the moon, offer her a unique mastery of dark and powerful sorceries.

A Rebellion Against Fate

Ranni's quest is to defy the gods, overthrow fate, and bring about an age of her own creation.

Conqueror of the Stars

Radahn, Scourge of the Stars

A Giant Amongst Warriors

General Radahn, one of the fiercest demigods, is a towering warrior who commands the might of both the stars and the battlefield. His mastery over gravity magic allowed him to halt the very stars in the sky, a testament to his unparalleled strength. A true champion, Radahn’s legend is carved in the annals of war.

Master of Gravity

Radahn's command of gravity magic is so potent, it allowed him to hold back the stars themselves.

Warrior's Steed

Though his size is immense, Radahn rides into battle on his loyal horse, Leonard, an inseparable companion.

Champion of the Golden Order

Radagon of the Golden Order

A Dual Identity

Radagon, the fierce champion of the Golden Order, embodies both strength and mystery. Once a formidable warrior, he ascended to become a god, embodying both the masculine and feminine divine as part of the mysterious relationship between Radagon and Queen Marika. His loyalty to the Golden Order and mastery of the sacred arts make him one of the most pivotal figures in The Lands Between.

Champion of Faith

Radagon's mastery of both martial and sacred arts made him the most revered champion of the Golden Order.

Divine Duality

Radagon and Queen Marika are revealed to share a divine connection, a mystery central to the Golden Order's teachings.

Lord of Blasphemy

Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy

Devoured by Ambition

Rykard, the lord of Mt. Gelmir, turned away from the Golden Order, seeking greater power through blasphemy. He allowed himself to be consumed by the great serpent, transforming into a monstrous being of both godlike power and unspeakable horror. His lust for dominance over the gods and his insatiable hunger led him down a dark path, where rebellion and sacrifice are his tools.

Blasphemous Serpent

Rykard's transformation into the Blasphemous Serpent symbolizes his complete rejection of the gods and his embrace of chaos.

A Path of Sacrifice

Through rebellion and sacrifice, Rykard amassed power that defies the Golden Order, but at great cost.
