About Elden Ring

An Epic Dark Fantasy Adventure

Elden Ring, the latest action RPG from the renowned developers FromSoftware, transports players to the expansive, lore-rich world of The Lands Between. Designed by Hidetaka Miyazaki and in collaboration with fantasy novelist George R. R. Martin, the game immerses players in a dark, mythical setting filled with intricate lore, formidable enemies, and challenging gameplay. Unravel the mysteries of the Elden Ring and discover your fate as you traverse stunning landscapes, face mighty bosses, and forge your path in a shattered world.

A Mythical Journey

Explore a vast open world brimming with deep lore, magical forces, and dark secrets hidden across The Lands Between.

Challenging Combat

Master the intricate combat system, fight epic bosses, and test your skills in a world that punishes mistakes but rewards perseverance.
